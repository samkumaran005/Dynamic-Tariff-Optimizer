Understanding Time of Use Tariffs and Smart Energy Management

What Are Time of Use Tariffs?

Time of Use (ToU) tariffs are pricing structures for electricity that vary based on the time of day. They offer lower rates during off-peak hours when electricity demand is lower, and higher rates during peak demand times when the grid is under stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Do I need a smart meter?

While smart meters make ToU billing easier, you can still benefit by manually tracking and scheduling your usage during off-peak hours based on our recommendations.

‚ùì Is ToU available in my area?

ToU tariffs are becoming increasingly common. Check with your local electricity provider to see if they offer time-based pricing plans.

‚ùì What appliances should I focus on?

Focus on high-power, schedulable appliances: washing machines, dishwashers, EV chargers, water heaters, pool pumps, and dryers. These offer the most savings potential.

Our calculations are based on standard ToU rates and your appliance specifications. Actual savings may vary based on your specific utility rates and usage patterns.
